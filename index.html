This is a tool to check your eligibility as a Registered nurse for the new AHPRA streamlined pathway.

What is new streamlined pathway?
The streamlined pathway is an easier and faster way for experienced overseas nurses to get Australia AHPRA registration without NCLEX and OSCE exams.

Important note: This tool is designed for candidates who have completed Nursing from India.
